About

JustSomeGamers is a community that sees everybody as just some gamer.
The simple idea is that we put friends and others as top-priority over games.
Here events and lobbies are created, supported, and hosted by members.

The heart of this community is talking and playing with eachother.
We use Mumble, it bring us together and access chats and lobbies
created by our friends and fellow members.

Members can create and lobby up in their own temporary channels.
Setting a custom status (private, friends only, and open) to their channels,
or find lobbies by using Looking 2 Play and Looking 2 Chat channels.

Every week members create and host their own private and public events.
They have complete creative and technical control over their events.
There-by, making this the first decentralized gaming community.
You can find all our events on our Steam Group's event's page.

Our Community Code are the 5 requirements for membership.
Every member is recorded reading our Code and uploaded to SoundCloud.
If you have your own recording on SoundCloud you can link us at our
Admin Contact, Youtube, Twitter, or Steam Group's comments.

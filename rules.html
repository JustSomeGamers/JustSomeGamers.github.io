JustSomeGamers has no sign-ups, no long agreement pages, and no interviews.


As a community, we want people who will stick with us for the long run.
To build a more trusting relationship on a social and individual level,
we decided to make things simple and have people read 5 sentences to join.


Once we have a record of your reading our Code once, that's it and you're in.

As to Code #3, there are exceptions for single player games, me-time, and playing with irl-friends. If you have any questions about the Code talk to anybody in the group.

Connecting

  1. After you have downloaded Mumble, follow the install wizard to completion.
  2. To connect to the server, click on the globe to the top left.
  3. Click on Add New... and new window will appear.
  4. You will be asked for our server info below.
  • Label: Just Some Gamers
  • Address: mumble.justsomegamers.com
  • Port: 54768
  • Username: Use your screen name

Questions & Answers

    • How do I get Mumble to stop changing the volume of my sound?
Go to Configure at the top of the Mumble window, and click on Settings in the drop down box. Next, in the bottom left hand corner, check the Advanced box. Now you have new options to look at. Finally click on Audio Output and uncheck the two boxes that say "while other users talk" and "while you talk".
    • How can I turn off Text-To-Speech?
Go to Configure at the top of the Mumble window, and click on Text-To-Speech in the drop down box. So long there is no check next to Text-To-Speech, you can enjoy your eardrums and brain cells for a little longer.
    • How do I set-up my microphone and sound?
Go to Configure at the top of the Mumble window, and click on Audio Wizard in the drop down box. Follow and configure the Audio Wizard to completion. Also, using push-to-talk makes you a good person.
    • Why was my connection refused?
This means you used the incorrect port. Double check the IP and Port on the connect screen.
    • Can I turn off the Mumble overlay?
Yes you can. Go to Configure at the top of the Mumble window, and click on Settings in the drop down box. Find Overlay to the left and click on it. Now you will see towards the top a check box titled "Enable Overlay". Uncheck that box and restart any apps that have the overlay currently. Once you restart those apps, the overlay will be removed.
